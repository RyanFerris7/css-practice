Lissa Brandon

,Salute fellow anthropoid

My name is Lissa Naomi Brandon and I’m a multidisciplinary artist. Since 2013 I’ve been focusing on the fashion industry, working firstly as a wardrobe stylist and later also as creative director. My passion lies in creating visual and tangible art, with people that share a similar vision. In the right space, I like to merge my creativity with my own face, by also operating in front of the lens. This is where my alterego Mojo Nodnarb exists. I aspire creative freedom by not putting a label or limit on my expressions. Besides styling, I’m currently open to collaborations with other brands through i.a. content creation, executing concepts and design.
